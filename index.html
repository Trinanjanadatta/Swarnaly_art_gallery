Hey There
I’m Swarnaly, a cheerful artist who loves making people happy with colors and patterns. I create fun Mandalas and beautiful Alpona designs, putting love and care into every piece.

I enjoy sharing my art, connecting with people, and spreading little moments of joy. Art for me is about creating something that makes your heart smile.

Thank you for visiting! I hope you enjoy exploring my gallery. There’s lots of colorful, heartfelt designs waiting for you. I am so glad you are here, can't wait to share this creative journey with you, one swirl at a time!
